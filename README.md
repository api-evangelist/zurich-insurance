## Zurich Insurance (zurich-insurance)

A Swiss multinational insurance company and one of the world's largest insurers providing property, casualty, life, and specialty insurance products. Zurich operates a public API platform at exchange.zurich.com (Zurich Exchange / ConnectZ / Zurich Edge) that exposes 30+ internal and external APIs across policy, claims, quoting, partner analytics, procurement, and operations.

**URL:** [https://www.zurich.com/](https://www.zurich.com/)

**API Exchange:** [https://exchange.zurich.com/](https://exchange.zurich.com/)
